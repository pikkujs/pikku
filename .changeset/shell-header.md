Add `ShellHeader`: a responsive single-bar page header that replaces the tall title + action-bar block. Title (first to collapse) and count on the left; filters, search, selection switch and actions on the right. Filters that don't fit collapse into a funnel → drawer (search is the last to fold), action labels degrade to icons, and the selection switch becomes a cycling button when narrow — all measured, not breakpointed. Also exports `PikkuSwitch`/`PikkuSwitchOption`.
